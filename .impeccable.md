## Design Context

### Users
Operators and administrators managing CCTV cameras, streams, and settings. They use the app in operational contexts where speed, clarity, and low cognitive load matter.

### Brand Personality
Executive, simple, clear. The interface should feel composed, trustworthy, and efficient rather than flashy. The tone is calm, direct, and controlled.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm editorial/product-led interface inspired by the provided Sangean reference: cream surfaces, walnut-brown framing, amber accents, rounded but disciplined geometry, and large confident typography. Avoid dark-neon dashboard styling, glassy overlays, and generic card-grid templates.

### Design Principles
- Prioritize clarity over decoration.
- Use warm neutrals with a single restrained accent color.
- Keep hierarchy strong with large titles and concise supporting text.
- Frame content in elegant panels rather than dense, cramped blocks.
- Preserve accessibility and keyboard usability while modernizing the look.
